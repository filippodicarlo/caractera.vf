Caractera is an old-style serif typeface inspired by the great classics such as Garamond and Minion Pro. Carefully designed to adapt to a variety of typographic contexts, this font is particularly suitable for long texts due to its refined style and flexibility.

The name “Caractera” phonically recalls Latin, a language deeply linked to the origins of typography. It is a tribute to the time when the first typefaces were developed for printing, inspired by the elegance and precision of humanistic script. This historical connection underscores the intent to blend tradition and innovation in a font that maintains the classic rigor of old-style serifs while offering the flexibility of variable technology.

The typeface's main feature is its variable structure on two axes: the first adjusts the weight, allowing a smooth transition from light to bold, while the second axis modifies the x-height of lowercase letters, optimizing readability at different sizes and contexts. This makes it perfect for both print design and the web, where maximum clarity and versatility is required.
